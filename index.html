Network Monitor.

Network monitor is your best application ever to know if you can access the Internet or not. It doesn't care if you're on WiFi, protected WiFi (such as hotels, planes and coffee shops) or corporate network. All that matters is that your computer can reach the Internet and that you know whether or not it's working!
